<p>There are several cafes near the base to regain the calories you burnt while climbing the mount. You can also relax at the beach or in the hot water spa. The Mount Maunganui Beachside Holiday Park is really popular for campers. It seemed to be packed when we visited the mount.</p>
<p>Mount Maunganui is about an hours drive from Rotorua and about 2-3 hours from Auckland.</p>
