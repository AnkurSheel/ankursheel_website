<h2 id="is-renting-a-waste-of-money"><a href="https://www.iwillteachyoutoberich.com/blog/renting-is-not-a-waste/">Is Renting a Waste of Money</a></h2>
<p><em>by <strong>Ramit Sethi</strong></em></p>
<p>Even though it's the same thing as paying to eat at a restaurant, many people believe that renting is throwing away money. This article shares the common reasons why believe the myth that renting is throwing away money.</p>
<h2 id="are-you-scared-of-not-buying-a-house"><a href="https://www.thehappysaver.com/blog/are-you-scared-of-not-buying-a-house">Are you scared of NOT buying a house?</a></h2>
<p><em>by <strong>Ruth</strong></em></p>
<p>A New Zealand centric article on the potential solutions for people struggling to buy a house in the NZ market. The article also goes into what's great and not so great about renting.</p>
<h2 id="how-i-built-a-modern-website-in-2021"><a href="https://kentcdodds.com/blog/how-i-built-a-modern-website-in-2021">How I built a modern website in 2021</a></h2>
<p><em>by <strong>Kent Dodds</strong></em></p>
<p>A blog post about how Kent rebuilt his website using modern tools. It gives an excellent overview of the architecture and tech used, so you can probably pick up some tidbits for your blog even if you don't have the same use case.</p>
<h2 id="getting-started-with-aws-cli-v2-as-a-docker-container"><a href="https://dev.to/aws-builders/getting-started-with-aws-cli-v2-as-a-docker-container-j23">Getting Started with AWS CLI v2 as a Docker Container</a></h2>
<p><em>by <strong>Fon Nkwenti</strong></em></p>
<p>Using the AWS CLI version 2 in a container-based environment means we don't have to manage the installation and dependencies ourselves.</p>
<h2 id="database-normalization"><a href="https://dev.to/fajarzuhrihadiyanto/database-normalization-jil">Database Normalization</a></h2>
