<html><head></head><body><p>Every week I come across some great articles. Here are some of the posts I feel you will enjoy reading as much as I did.</p>
<p><strong>Charity Majors</strong> shares her thought on the <a href="https://charity.wtf/2018/08/17/on-engineers-and-influence/">different type of influence that an engineer can have</a> and use.</p>
<p><strong>Alex</strong> demonstrates how he participated in the Do Not Get Arrested Challenge 2020 by <a href="https://mango.pdf.zone/finding-former-australian-prime-minister-tony-abbotts-passport-number-on-instagram">logging in as the former Australian Primer minister Tony Abbot on an airline website</a> and got details such as his passport id after he shared a photo of his boarding pass on Instagram. It's a long but entertaining story.</p>
<p><strong>Jared Palmer</strong> has an excellent <a href="https://jaredpalmer.com/gatsby-vs-nextjs">takedown of the Gatsby Framework</a> and why he has moved away from Gatsby to Next.js. This blog is <a href="migrating-from-wordpress-to-gatsby">built on gatsby</a>, and I find it difficult to debug issues and add functionality. At least, now I know it's just not me.</p>
<p>How do you bootstrap a SASS company to 1 million ARR? <strong>Sarah Hum</strong> breaks down the <a href="https://canny.io/blog/how-we-built-a-1m-arr-saas-startup/">learnings from Canny's 3.5-year journey by revenue/hiring milestones</a>. So many tidbits to take away if you are thinking of starting a SASS business.</p>
<p>We, as humans, find it difficult to talk about size. After all, how do you define big and small? <strong>Tim Urban</strong> takes the full-sized galaxies and the subatomic particles and shows the <a href="https://waitbutwhy.com/2020/09/universe.html">size by scaling them into numbers we can understand</a>. The post has some lovely illustrations to drive the point home.</p>
<p>A lot of successful people feel like crap all the time. But what if you could make your day more enjoyable and still be wealthy and successful. <strong>Andrew Wilkinson</strong> shares his list of <a href="https://medium.com/@awilkinson/the-power-of-anti-goals-c38f5f46d23c">Anti-goals and how you can come up with the perfect day</a> by thinking about your worst day and then figuring out how to avoid it</p>
<p>Hope you enjoy reading these articles as much as I did.</p>
