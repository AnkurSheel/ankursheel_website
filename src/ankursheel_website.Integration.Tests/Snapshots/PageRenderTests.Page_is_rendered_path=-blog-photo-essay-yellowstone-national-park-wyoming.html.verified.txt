<html><head></head><body><p>From the Corn Palace, we now move to Yellowstone National Park.</p>
<p>It is known for its wildlife, geothermal features and the Old Faithful Geyser. This is one of those places that I believe should be on your itinerary.</p>
<p>It is a beautiful place, and pictures cannot do justice (even though I have tried). If you are planning a day trip, try to be at Old Faithful just before it is scheduled to erupt.</p>
<p>Even though we were unaware of the timings, we were lucky enough to reach the geyser just a few minutes before the eruption.</p>
