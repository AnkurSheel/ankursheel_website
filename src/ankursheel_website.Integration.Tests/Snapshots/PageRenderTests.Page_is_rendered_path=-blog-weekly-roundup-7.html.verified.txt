<html><head></head><body><p>Every week I come across some great articles. Here are some of the posts I feel you will enjoy reading as much as I did.</p>
<p>Do you feel that your house is cluttered with junk? <strong>Emma</strong> from <strong>Money can buy me happiness</strong> has a list of <a href="http://www.moneycanbuymehappiness.com/things-to-get-rid-of-right-now/">47 things to get rid of</a> to de-clutter your home and life.</p>
<p>The first million dollars is supposed to be the hardest. But is there something more challenging? <strong>J.Money</strong> from <strong>Budgets Are Sexy</strong> explains why getting to your first <a href="http://www.budgetsaresexy.com/2016/10/the-first-100-thousand-is-the-hardest/">100,000 is the hardest</a>.</p>
<p>Does the number of items that you own correlate to your happiness? <strong>Chris</strong> from <strong>Keep Thrifty</strong> shows how they are <a href="https://www.keepthrifty.com/2016/10/10/become-twice-as-happy-with-half-as-much/">twice as happy with half as much</a>.</p>
<p>Home-ownership in New Zealand is a hot topic. <strong>NZMuse</strong> discusses why <a href="http://nzmuse.com/2016/10/can-really-afford-not-buy-house/#comment-1436065">New Zealand is a place where you can't afford to buy a house, and yet you can't afford NOT to</a>, either.</p>
<p>Have you been wondering how to get started with saving for the future? <strong>Allan</strong> from <strong>The Practical Saver</strong> gives the <a href="http://www.thepracticalsaver.com/keys-building-financial-foundation/">keys for building a financial foundation</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Gavin</strong> from <strong>Zen Pencils</strong> has a new <a href="http://zenpencils.com/comic/beethoven/">comic with an inspirational quote from Ludwig Van Beethoven</a>.</p>
<p>Conventional wisdom tells us to make ourselves indispensable at work. <strong>Lihsuan</strong> from <strong>8th Light</strong> tells us why <a href="https://8thlight.com/blog/lihsuan-lung/2016/10/12/make-yourself-dispensable.html">striving to be dispensable</a> is a good thing.</p>
<p>Om has a new <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/ux-review-marvel-contest-champions-om-tandon">UX review for Marvel Contest Of Champions</a> on his Linkedin page.</p>
<p>Hope you enjoy reading these articles as much as I did.</p>
