<html><head></head><body><p>Every week I come across some great articles. Here are some of the posts I feel you will enjoy reading as much as I did.</p>
<p><del>How can you use Google Sheets to post a new tweet every day? <strong>Alberto Montalesi</strong> has an article that shows how to send daily automated tweets from a Google Sheet</del>.</p>
<p>Tired of reading articles to find inspiration for your product and design challenges. <strong>Dan Benoni</strong> and <strong>Louis-Xavier Lavallee</strong> have created an <a href="https://growth.design/case-studies/">entertaining way to learn from the best companies to design and grow products users love</a>.</p>
<p>At some point in time, we have all made the mistake of committing a secret to a Github Repository. <strong>Michael Crump</strong> shows how you can <a href="https://dev.to/mbcrump/remove-passwords-committed-to-github-on-accident-1oj9">remove secrets from a Github repo</a>.</p>
<p>CSS Grid and CSS Flexbox - 2 of the most popular layout models. <strong>Thomas De Moor</strong> talks about the <a href="https://dev.to/x-team/css-grid-vs-flexbox-3pp6">differences between the 2 models and which one to choose</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Nick Maggiulli</strong> talks about the different Levels of Wealth and how including <a href="https://ofdollarsanddata.com/climbing-the-wealth-ladder/">lifestyle adjustments based on your current level</a> can be highly rewarding.</p>
<p>Hope you enjoy reading these articles as much as I did.</p>
