<html><head></head><body><p>For our very next escape, we travel through Northland to the the very tip of New Zealand - the place where the Tasman Sea meets the mighty Pacific Ocean.</p>
<p>Travelling on an empty stomach is hard and the road is long. Luckily, our first stop is at the Matakana Farmers market and we are just in time for breakfast.</p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-1.jpg" alt="Ducks in the parking lot"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-2.jpg" alt="Local Entertainment at Matakana's Farmer market"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-3.jpg" alt="Matakana Farmers Market"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-4.jpg" alt="And there they go"></p>
<p>After filling our stomachs temporarily, we start off on our journey again only to reach our next stop at the Whangarei Falls Loop.</p>
<p>Whangarei Falls is a picturesque waterfall, falling vertically for 26.3 metres over basalt cliffs. Its a short walk from the parking lot to the waterfall. There is also a flight of steps to help reach the base of the waterfall safely.</p>
<p>The surprise of finding a picnic table at the base, made it the perfect spot to have lunch.</p>
<p>If you are the adventurous type, you can also take an extremely slippery path to go behind the waterfall. Its not very difficult, considering that my friends 9 year old daughter was able to join us easily.</p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-5.jpg" alt="The Whangarei Falls"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-6.jpg" alt="Swimming in front of The Falls"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-7.jpg" alt="Quack Quack"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-8.jpg" alt="Kids Jumping into the water"></p>
<p>Next we take a quick break at the Cable Bay beach while wait for the rest of our group to catch up.</p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-10.jpg" alt="Enjoying the water at Cable Bay"></p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-11.jpg" alt="I am still standing, just"></p>
<p>And we are off again to our final destination - Cape Reinga.</p>
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/new-zealand-escapes-northland-part-1/CapeReinga-9.jpg" alt="The view from the road"></p>
<p>In the next part of the series, we will continue our journey to Cape Reinga and make our way back to Auckland.</p>
